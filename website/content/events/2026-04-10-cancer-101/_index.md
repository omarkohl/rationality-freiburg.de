## What will we do?

Cancer isn't as simple as it sounds and different types behave in very different
ways. But they all share the same roots: something goes wrong in how cells grow
and divide, and the body's normal defenses fail to catch it.

This talk breaks down how that happens, why it often takes years to develop, what
the real risk factors are, and what modern treatments are actually doing.
