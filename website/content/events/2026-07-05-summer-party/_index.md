After a short break last year, we are happy to announce the details for our summer party this year!

## When and Where

**Date:** Sunday, July 5th  
**Location:** Stadtgarten, Freiburg  
**Start time:** 15:00

## What to Bring

We will be providing the drinks, so all you need to bring is your favorite summer dish to share! Whether it's a savory snack, a sweet treat, or a delicious main course, your contribution will help make this party a hit.

To keep things organized, we will create a Google Doc where you can add what food item you're planning to bring. You can also note your preferred drinks, and we will do our best to fulfill your wishes. The link will be shared in the Signal messenger group a week before the event.

Since the party will be held in a park, please bring picnic blankets, your own cups/cutlery, and your favorite games to add to the fun!

## Questions

If you have any questions or need further details, don't hesitate to reach out to **info@rationality-freiburg.de**.

Looking forward to celebrating with you all!
